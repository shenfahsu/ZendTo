ZendTo 

It is a classic problem: you need to transfer files to someone, or they need to transfer them to you, and there's no way except email. But they are too large or your administrator won't let you transfer the files by email at all. ZendTo to the rescue!

This is a web-based package that will let you provide a secure and easy-to-use method for your users to be able to exchange files with other people, even with people on other sites that do not have any login rights at your site.

It is based on the "Dropbox" package written by the University of Delaware. I am eternally grateful to them.
